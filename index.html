The Method

Eight D's, from idea to impact.

Every engagement runs through one repeatable framework — so nothing falls through the cracks between strategy and shipping.

  1. 1

    Discover

    Understand the problem, users and market. Validate the real need before a line of code.

  2. 2

    Discuss

    Align stakeholders on goals, constraints and success metrics.

  3. 3

    Define

    Turn intent into a sharp scope, roadmap and prioritised backlog.

  4. 4

    Design

    Shape the experience — flows, interface and architecture.

  5. 5

    Develop

    Build in tight iterations with working software every step.

  6. 6

    Deliver

    Ship to production, instrument it, and get it into real hands.

  7. 7

    Data

    Measure behaviour and outcomes — let evidence steer decisions.

  8. 8

    Drive

    Iterate, grow and pivot. Keep momentum until it compounds.
